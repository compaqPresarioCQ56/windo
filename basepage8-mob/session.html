How to Operate?

This version of Windo 7/8 is designed for touch-screen devices, such as smartphones, tablets, or touch-compatible laptops.
To navigate the interface. Press the K+ button to see a list of all available apps.
By default, any open and running apps are listed next to the K+ button on the taskbar.
Press Active to reduce the running apps clutter.
To hide the bar fully, press the ▽ button at the window titlebar.
Close 0and minimize are quite self explanatory.

- If you want to go back to the desktop interface, click here (just click BACK on the first thing!)
